Regime agent decision flow — how agents buy, sell, and hold

Annotated map of the production decision path. Stage numbers in the diagram correspond to the module annotations in the table below.

Stage annotations — where each step lives in the code

StageWhat happensCode
1 · Stock identificationUniverse screen (≥$5, ≥756 trading days history, dollar-ADV floor, no leveraged/FX/bond funds) feeds discovery, which flags Bull-regime candidates with probability ≥ 0.55 and crowd score ≤ 40 onto the watchlist as "Entry Signal". Candidates route to all four agents; each applies its mandate filter.universe.py · discovery.py · agent_candidate_intake.py · agent_policy.agent_candidate_policy
2 · Signal enginePer ticker daily: walk-forward 3-state HMM (504-day window, refit every 21 days, multi-seed agreement scoring) labels the regime; the transition matrix projects 21-day forward probabilities; technicals overlay; the composite signal emits buy/hold/sell with strength. The meta-labeler scores P(success) on managed-exit labels — calibrated, and only allowed to influence decisions if its out-of-fold AUC ≥ 0.55.hmm_engine.fit_regime_model · signals.signal_from_forward_curve · signals.build_composite_signal · meta_labeler.MetaLabelerEngine.analyze
3 · Entry gatesA buy candidate must pass every gate: VIX freeze (≥35 halts buys), signal freshness ≤ 3 days and price-drift check, anti-churn round-trip cap, hurdle rate net of execution cost and short-term tax, regime-duration minimum, earnings blackout ≤ 2 days, portfolio drawdown pause at −5%, and HMM seed agreement. Any failure → no entry; the agent holds cash.paper_trading/planning.generate_buy_plans · signal_quality.py · anti_churn.py · hurdle_rate.py · vix_freeze.py · agent_policy.buy_pause_status
4 · Size & executeSize = how many shares a 2×ATR stop can lose within the risk budget (2% of portfolio), scaled 0.5–1.0× by the meta-labeler's calibrated probability, dampened by beta and concentration. The plan is auto-approved by threshold, then broker guardrails (price collars, max position 10%, daily loss limits, trades/day) validate before the IBKR limit order. Stop/target geometry is recomputed from the actual routed fill price.paper_trading/sizing._risk_adjusted_quantity · paper_trading/planning._actual_fill_trade_geometry · broker_adapter.validate_guardrails · order_routing.decide_routing
5 · Daily managementThe exit ladder runs daily in strict priority: profit target → stop/trailing stop (close-based ratchet: activates 1 ATR above entry, trails at 2×ATR, never moves down) → 21-calendar-day time stop → regime flip to Bear or composite Sell → Bull-to-Neutral deterioration sells half. If nothing triggers, the agent holds and ratchets the stop. The LTCG tax override may trim sells protecting lots near long-term status. Closed positions are logged and feed meta-labeler retraining.paper_trading/planning.generate_exit_plans · paper_trading/planning.trailing_stop_level · ltcg_override.py · paper_trading/performance.record_trade_outcome
