Account numbers are stored masked (last4 only). Avoid storing original rows unless you explicitly enable “Store redacted original row JSON”.
How dedupe works
Exact dedupe uses a stable transaction hash. “Fuzzy dedupe” also checks date ±1 day, amount, and merchant to avoid double imports across overlapping statements.
